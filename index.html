Our Services

Complete Auto Repair &
Maintenance Under One Roof

From routine oil changes to complex engine repairs, Le's Auto Repair handles it all. We work on all makes and models with honest pricing and quality parts.

Oil Change & Lube

Keep your engine running smooth with fast, affordable oil changes. We use premium synthetic and conventional oils and never push unnecessary add-ons. Just what your car needs.

Brake Service & Repair

Squeaking, grinding, or a soft pedal? We diagnose the issue accurately and show you exactly what needs replacing. No scare tactics. Just honest, safe brake repairs at a fair price.

Engine Repair

Check engine light on? Our experienced technicians diagnose and repair engine issues of every complexity. From sensors and gaskets to full rebuilds, we get it running right.

Smog Check

Stay legal and pass emissions with confidence. Le's is a certified smog check station. We test, diagnose failures, and repair the issue so you can get your registration renewed.

Transmission Service

Hard shifts, slipping, or leaks? We service both automatic and manual transmissions. Get an honest assessment before spending thousands at the dealership. We save you money.

Electrical & Battery

Dead battery, faulty alternator, or mysterious electrical problems? We trace the root cause, explain it clearly, and fix it right the first time. No guesswork, just results.

Tire Service

Flat tire, uneven wear, or need a new set? We offer tire repair, rotation, balancing, and competitive pricing on new tires. Keep your ride safe and smooth without overpaying.

General Maintenance

Keep your car running at its best with scheduled maintenance. Fluid flushes, filter replacements, belt inspections, and more. Preventive care saves you money down the road.
